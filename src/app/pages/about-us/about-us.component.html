Who We Are

Inventive. Proactive. Professional.

It was in 2004 that Agents of Value had its humble beginnings. We started out as a virtual staffing company, providing dedicated agents to clients from countries across different continents, but mainly from the USA, UK, Australia, South Africa, and some countries in Asia. Back then, the tasks assigned to our agents were internet marketing, web content development, SEO, web design and development, and virtual assistance. Today, more than a decade after, Agents of Value has achieved several milestones. From one small office in Makati in the Philippines, the company has expanded and now has two more offices for its operations in strategic locations in the country. And from staff leasing, the company has evolved into a full-fledged tech solutions provider with over a hundred agents who are skilled IT professionals, ready to serve and meet industry demands.
